Idaho Rafting Excursions

Welcome to Idaho Rafting Excursions, where adventure meets the untamed beauty of nature. Since our founding in 2020, we have been dedicated to providing thrilling and unforgettable white water rafting experiences for adventurers of all ages. We have excursions that will satisfy any type of adventurer. We hope that you will be able to join us for a rafting adventure soon!

History

At Idaho Rafting Adventures, our journey has been marked by passion, dedication, and a shared love for the outdoors. Founded in 2020, our company emerged with the idea of creating a company where people of all ages and thrill seekers of all types could have an adventure tailored towards them. We started out small only offering a few different excursions. As our expertise and company grew, we expanded and added even more excursions to help us to cater to an ever growing rafting industry. Our journey is not just about the water though, we strive to make connections with each and every guest that steps foot into one of our rafts. Our staff are extremely hospitable and love to interact with people of all different backgrounds. We know that you will enjoy your trip with us!
